Introduction to COVID-19

Today the whole world is facing a pandemic situation due to a virus named COVID-19.Now lets know about waht is it,how generated etc. Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses that are known to cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). Coronavirus (COVID-19) was identified in 2019 in Wuhan, China. This is a new coronavirus that has not been previously identified in humans. The World Health Organisation (WHO) has declared the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) a pandemic. A global coordinated effort is needed to stop the further spread of the virus.

What is Coronavirus?

Coronaviruses are a family of viruses that cause illness such as respiratory diseases or gastrointestinal diseases. Respiratory diseases can range from the common cold to more severe diseases.

Symptoms of COVID :

Among those who will become infected ,following symptoms will be seen :-

*Respiratory symptoms

*Fever

*Cough

*Breathing difficulties

*Fatigue

*Sore throat


The virus that causes COVID-19 infects people of all ages. However, evidence to date suggests that three groups of people are at a higher risk of getting severe COVID-19 disease.

1.People over 70 years of age

2.People with serious chronic illnesses

3.People who are physically inactive


Preventions

The WHO suggests the following basic preventative measures to protect against the new coronavirus.

1.Stay up to date with the latest information on the COVID-19 outbreak through WHO.

2.Perform hand hygiene frequently with an alcohol-based hand rub.

3.Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

4.Maintain social distancing (approximately 2 meters) from individuals .

5.Wear a mask.
